Ingredients:
CCK chicken ball
oil, for shallow frying
1/3 cup Japanese mirin, sweet rice wine
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon sugar
white sesame, optional
scallion , or cilantro, chopped, optional

Instructions:
Step 1:Heat a pan or skillet with some oil and shallow-fry the meatballs until they are lightly browned and cooked through.

Step 2: Remove the excess oil from the pan or skillet. Lower the heat to medium-low and add the Japanese mirin, soy sauce, and sugar. Stir well with the chicken meatballs. Simmer and cook until the sauce slightly thickens, about 5 minutes.

Step 3: Top with sesame seeds and chopped scallions or cilantro, if desired. Dish out and serve immediately.
